Who we are

JantaPro is an online employment solution for people who can offer hands on skills such as techincians, plumbers, electricians, artisans and builders seeking jobs and the employers who need great people. We want to become a global provider of a full array of job seeking, career management, recruitment and talent management products and services. At the heart of our success and our future is innovation: We are changing the way people think about this type of work, and we're helping...them actively improve their lives and their workforce performance with new technology,tools and practices. As we continue to seek strategic ways to expand our offerings to employers and seekers, we have taken steps to ensure that we have the process, technology and infrastruxture to deliver the highest level of service and support to our customers.
